Purpose of the game:

Our program will calculate the flow of money of a user as a lot of people do not keep track of their daily expenses and their income/s. The user gets their daily report with the stats provided, in order to maintain their over expense and manage money.

People’s budgeting skills are detrimental to their future. That's why we plan to teach our users ways to better manage their money. Following a budget or spending plan will also keep our wusers out of debt and will help them work out any debt they currently have.



Our Goal:

The main reason behind us creating this application is to help find a way to help people budget but we want to make it feel fun so it does not feel like a chore to our users. Nowadays nearly 3 out of 5 Americans struggle managing money. This is where our application comes in and helps with that.
